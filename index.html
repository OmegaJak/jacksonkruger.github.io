Jimstown (C#/Unity)

I made a game in Unity with 4 friends in 48 hours for the 2019 Global Game Jam. It was heavily inspired by the game Jamestown. I then built an AI that planned its motion in this game to survive as long as possible using different planning algorithms. This was my final project for CSCI4511W ("Introduction to Artificial Intelligence") at the U of M.
